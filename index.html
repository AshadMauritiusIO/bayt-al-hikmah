Engine Subsystem 01

Islamic Knowledge Hub

Primary Source Disciplines

Core foundational disciplines mapped into distinct evolutionary branches to provide a structured pathway from basic functional literacy up to advanced computational scholarship.

01
العقيدة

Aqeedah (Theology & Creed)

  • ▪
    Foundations of Faith: Comprehensive overview of the core articles of Islamic belief (Allah, His Angels, His Books, His Messengers, the Last Day, and Divine Decree).
  • ▪
    Asma was-Sifat: Analytical explanations detailing the Names and Perfect Attributes of God.
  • ▪
    Theological Studies: Comparative evaluation of classical frameworks to preserve authentic, mainstream creeds.
02
علوم القرآن

Quranic Sciences (Ulum al-Quran)

  • ▪
    Tafsir (Exegesis): Structural, analytical, grammatical, and contextual explanations of Quranic verses.
  • ▪
    Tajweed & Qira'at: Phonetic articulation rules, precision pronunciation, and verified modes of recitation.
  • ▪
    Asbab al-Nuzul: Historical contextualization maps detailing specific triggers behind verse revelations.
03
علوم الحديث

Hadith Sciences (Ulum al-Hadith)

  • ▪
    Mustalah al-Hadith: Structural methodologies utilized to analyze chain authenticity and grading frameworks.
  • ▪
    Textual Studies: Textual analysis of primary collections (the Six Books and Imam Nawawi’s Forty Hadith).
04
الفقه وأصوله

Jurisprudence (Fiqh & Usul)

  • ▪
    Fiqh al-Ibadat: Rulings governing daily actions and ritual worship (purification, prayer, fasting, zakat, and pilgrimage).
  • ▪
    Fiqh al-Muamalat: Commercial contracts, operational finance laws, inheritance, and family law parameters.
  • ▪
    Usul al-Fiqh: Methodologies used by scholars to extract specific rulings directly from primary legislative materials.
05
السيرة والتاريخ

History & Seerah (Tarikh)

  • ▪
    The Seerah: Chronological academic exploration of the life, immaculate character, and prophetic mission of Prophet Muhammad.
  • ▪
    Tarikh: Historical study of the Rightly Guided Caliphs, evolutionary expansion architectures, and early empires.
06
التزكية والأخلاق

Spirituality & Ethics (Tazkiyah)

  • ▪
    Tazkiyat al-Nafs: Purification paradigms tracking metrics for character development and internal spiritual diagnostics.
  • ▪
    Akhlaq: Structural study of prophetic attributes, moral philosophies, and social ethics parameters.
07
اللغة العربية

Arabic Language (Lughah)

Nahu (Syntax) Classical Arabic sentence structuring and structural case ending rules.
Sarf (Morphology) Word origin architecture, derivation structures, and transformation systems.
Balaqah (Rhetoric) Classical linguistic eloquence, literary devices, and complex semantic layers.
