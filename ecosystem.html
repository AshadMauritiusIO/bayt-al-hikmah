Core Modules & Features

Comprehensive functional blocks built inside the structural system application ecosystem.

1. Operations & Prayer Management

  • Iqamah Scheduler: Easily update the gap between Adhan and Iqamah. Changes instantly sync to the masjid's public app/website and TV screens.
  • Jummah Speaker Rota: A calendar interface to assign Khateebs (speakers) for Friday prayers, manage their honorariums, and store sermon topics.

2. Financial Hub (Donations & Zakat)

  • Donation Tracker: Categorized incoming funds (General Sadaqah, Zakat, Mosque Expansion, Orphan Fund).
  • Kiosk/POS Integration: Manage the digital donation kiosks located in the musallah.
  • Financial Transparency Reports: One-click generation of PDF expense and income reports for the board of trustees and community transparency.

3. Madrasah & Education Portal

  • Student Roster: Profiles for children and adults enrolled in Quranic Arabic, Hifz, or Islamic studies.
  • Attendance & Progress: Digital logbooks for teachers to track Juz/Surah memorization progress.
  • Tuition Invoicing: Automated monthly fee collection and waiver management for families in need.

4. Community Services & Social Welfare

  • Zakat Disbursement: A secure portal to review applications from families seeking financial assistance, tracking case history and distributions.
  • Matrimonial & Nikah Registry: Digital booking system for marriage ceremonies, document uploads (IDs, marriage licenses), and Imam assignments.
  • Funeral (Janazah) Coordination: Quick-action dashboard to coordinate with funeral homes, log burial plots, and blast community announcements.

Super Admin Full Access Board members / Head Administrator. Can view finances, manage users, and change system settings.
Imam / Khateeb Content & Community Can manage prayer times, education modules, and view matrimonial/counseling requests.
Treasurer Financials Only Full access to Donations, Zakat applications, and expense logging. No access to student grades.
Volunteer Operations Only Can check in event attendees, update community bulletin drafts, and log facility maintenance issues.
